Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

InkManager Pro is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data in our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

InkManager Pro is designed with privacy in mind. We collect and store:

Data You Provide Directly:

  • Client Information: Names, contact details, birth dates, skin types, medical notes
  • Session Data: Appointment details, tattoo designs, pricing, session notes
  • Inventory Data: Product names, quantities, suppliers, pricing
  • Business Data: Revenue, schedules, professional notes

Automatically Collected Information:

  • App Usage Data: Feature usage, preferences, settings
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data exclusively to:

  • Provide core app functionality (client management, scheduling, inventory tracking)
  • Improve user experience and app performance
  • Generate business reports and analytics
  • Maintain app security and prevent fraud
  • Provide customer support

3. Data Storage & Security

Local Storage:

All your data is stored locally on your device using secure browser storage. We don't transmit your data to external servers unless you explicitly use export/backup features.

Data Export:

When you export data (backup functionality), files are generated locally on your device. You control where these files are stored and shared.

Security Measures:

  • Data encryption in local storage
  • Secure data handling practices
  • Regular security updates

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We Do Not Sell Your Data: InkManager Pro does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Limited Sharing: Your data may be shared only in these circumstances:

  • When you explicitly use export features
  • If required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights or users' safety

5. Your Rights & Control

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: View all your data within the app
  • Modification: Edit or update your information anytime
  • Deletion: Remove clients, sessions, or inventory items
  • Export: Download all your data in JSON format
  • App Uninstall: Removing the app deletes all local data

6. International Data Transfers

Since data is stored locally on your device, no international data transfers occur unless you explicitly export and share data across devices.

7. Children's Privacy

InkManager Pro is intended for professional tattoo artists and studio owners. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is not designed for or directed at children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date and, if significant, through in-app notifications.

9. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: privacy@inkmanagerpro.com

Support: support@inkmanagerpro.com

We typically respond to privacy-related inquiries within 2-3 business days.

10. Compliance

InkManager Pro is designed to comply with major privacy regulations including:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
  • Google Play Store data safety requirements
